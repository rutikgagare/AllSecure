Health Emergency

A health crisis or public health crisis is a difficult situation or complex health system that affects humans in one or more geographic areas (mainly occurred in natural hazards). Health crisis generally have significant impacts on community health, loss of life, and on the economy. We cannot treat our way out of these problems. Preventing suffering before it happens is the long-term answer for our country. Our goal is to create a community where everybody has opportunity to thrive.
